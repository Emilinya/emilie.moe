The best lesbian media

This is a list of the subjectively best lesbian media. Note that i exclusively consume anime-adjacent media, so don't expect to see anything else. The list is in order from best to worst, but I recommend everything in this list.

Adachi and Shimamura (Ongoing)

Synopsis

Adachi and Shimamura, two young women who attend the same high school, are inseparable friends. Whether playing table tennis, chatting about favorite TV shows, or just relaxing together, they're happy to share their days. When Adachi's friendship turns into romantic attraction, the relationship begins to change, one day at a time.

Comments

Very cute slow-burn slice of life, do not expect any rapid developments. This story is adapted into an anime, but I recommend reading the original light novels. You can probably find the light novels online for free somewhere, or you could buy them from bookdepository.com. I own all the published books physically, so if you know me in real life, I would be happy to let you borrow them.

Mage & Demon Queen (Ongoing)

Synopsis

Adventurers seek to take the demon queen’s head, but a love-struck young female mage wishes to take her hand. Join us won’t you, for this bawdy tale of love and persistence set inside a real-life RPG.

Comments

A funny love story. It start off very silly, but becomes more serious eventually. It also features a trans princess as a side character. The story is available for free at WebToons

Lily (Ongoing)

Synopsis

Prim and proper honors student Fan Yilin and the mysterious and charismatic Lan Ruoxi met through a stroke of fate and a misunderstanding, and only got further involved with each other from that point on. The more they came to know each other, the more something different started blooming between the two. "I like you, only because it's you."

Comments

This is an exceptionally slow-burn slice of life romance. The main pair does eventually become a couple, but it takes several hundred chapters. The story is officially published by Bilibili Comics, but is also available for free at MangaDex. While I do recommend supporting the creator, the reading experience on Bilibili Comics is worse than on MangaDex. There are also a lot more translated chapters on MangaDex, so you do need to switch over eventually.

Bloom Into You (Finished)

Synopsis

Yuu has always loved shoujo manga and awaits the day she gets a love confession that sends her heart aflutter with bubbles and hearts, and yet when a junior high classmate confesses his feelings to her…she feels nothing. Disappointed and confused, Yuu enters high school still unsure how to respond. That’s when Yuu sees the beautiful student council president Nanami turn down a suitor with such maturity that she’s inspired to ask her for help. But when the next person to confess to Yuu is Nanami herself, has her shoujo romance finally begun?

Comments

This is one of the most famous lesbian manga. It is adapted into an anime, which covers about half of the story. The anime is very good, so I would recommend watching that initially. I own this manga physically, but am missing volumes four, six and seven. There exists a series of spin-off novels written by the author of Adachi and Shimamura, which I also own.

Not so Shoujo Love Story (Ongoing)

Synopsis

Romance-super-fan Rei Chan is ready for her first boyfriend and she knows just who it'll be: the most handsome boy in school, Hansum Ochinchin. But her plans for the perfect love story are derailed when the most popular girl in class declares herself a rival....for Rei's heart?! This is the year her not so shoujo love story begins!

Comments

This is a very silly, but also very cute, comic. The main character suffers from comphet, so it takes a while for her to understand her feelings. The story is also available for free at WebToons

Hana ni Arashi (Ongoing)

Synopsis

Nanoha and Chidori are students at an all-girls school who are dating but trying to keep this a secret from all of their classmates.

Comments

This is a very cute slice of life story. It is not picked up by any english publisher, so, unless you can read Japanese, the only place to read it is MangaDex.

Pulse (Finished)

Synopsis

Mel, a renowned heart surgeon, lives a carefree life with sex being a tool for joy rather than a show of affection. Then she meets someone that turns her view of love and life upside down. This story is about two people that meet with minimal expectations but soon become enthralled in a relationship that changes everything about themselves.

Comments

This comic has a lot of explicit lesbian sex, so keep that in mind. It is very dramatic, which I typically don't like, but the main couple is very cute, and it does have a happy ending. You can buy it physically at bookdepository.com, or read it on MangaBuddy.

Can't Defy the Lonely Girl (Finished)

Synopsis

Ayaka Sakurai is an excellent student, but she gets nervous at exams, so she had to settle for a lesser high school than she expected. However, her teacher offered Ayaka a recommendation letter to the school of her choice if she could convince the delinquent girl Sora Honda to come to school. When Ayaka visited Sora at her home, Sora was quite willing to go back to school, if Ayaka would agree to do one thing...

Comments

The relationship between the main characters feels a bit coercive in the beginning, but that gets resolved, and the relationship becomes reciprocal. This story is also not picked up by any english publisher, so you have to read it on MangaDex.

Anemone Is in Heat (Ongoing)

Synopsis

Nagisa Ootsuki shed her former self out of shame from failing her highschool entrance exams. On her first day in her new highschool, who does she meet but the girl who made her fail the exam! How does Nagisa deal with these complicated emotions?

Comments

Yet another story with no english publisher, available on MangaDex.

Wife & Wife (Finished)

Synopsis

Sumi and Kinana have just moved in together. This is the refreshing and hilarious story of their (romantic) everyday life.

Comments

This is a short and sweet story about a couple moving in together. Read it at on MangaDex.

Cheerful Amnesia (Finished)

Synopsis

Mari's girlfriend, Arisa, loses her memories of the past three years, including all memory of their life together. However, it turns out that being crazy in love with someone transcends all realms of possibility.

Comments

This manga is a bit too unserious for my tastes, but is is still cute. Read it on MangaDex.
